Telling stories through play and making experiences with make believe people. Over the years, I’ve found myself deeply entrenched in games’ narratives, dabbling and diving into their tech and systems, and designing to put the player’s agency and self-expression first and foremost. For the past few years I’ve been working with the incredible teams at Larian Studios working on the recently announced Divinity and previously the critically acclaimed and player-lauded Baldur’s Gate 3. Prior to that I was working on a few projects either as a hobbyist or in a freelance capacity. As time marches ever onward I learn more and more about both my own craft of storytelling and the ways the medium of games can be pushed to tell different stories.

Larian Studios
